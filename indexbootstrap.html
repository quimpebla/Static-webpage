Recording studio in Barcelona

Koala Studios is the recording studio of Alberto Quintero, composer and musical producer of national and international artists, with more than 30 years of experience in the sector, more than 20 million records sold worldwide, and millions of listeners accumulated on YouTube and Spotify. We offer you a warm and welcoming atmosphere in one of the best recording studios in the city. We want you to feel comfortable and can give free rein to your creativity, and guided by our tech team, can get the sound you are looking for in your projects, regardless of your musical style or budget.

Services

Music Production

Get in our facilities the type of sound that others look for in recording studios outside of Spain. Raise the quality of your project in a professional recording studio, which allows you to show it in an increasingly competitive market and sell it on different digital platforms.

Recording

Professional-quality digital audio recording with ProTools HDX, high-quality digital converters and preamps, and the best microphones for all kinds of musical and audiovisual productions. Our 15-meter room with variable acoustics allows recording any style and group of musicians.

Mix

Get a mix that can compete on the same level as commercial mixes. It is not true that everything is fixed in the mix, but it is true that everything can be spoiled in a bad mix. We are your best option if you are looking for quality and your work aspires to compete with other artists on iTunes, Spotify, or YouTube.

Mastering Online

Do you need to make a DDP, Mastering for iTunes or Spotify? We master with original analog equipment and the latest digital outboard. Send us your tracks, album, EP, or single for mastering, and we will deliver it the same day. Call us, and we will send you a quote within 24 hours.

Musical arrangement

Do you need musical arrangements? Record strings, and you’re not a musician or you don’t know anything about orchestra or sheet music? We have been orchestrating and arranging songs for artists inside and outside of Spain for more than 30 years. We will record any instrument, and we will create the score and the parts to record with a symphony orchestra.

Voice overs

Looking for a voice to speak for your brand or make your podcast shine? We have a wide range of professional voice talents in several languages, different age ranges, and voice types, and with immediate availability, even on the same day, to perform any radio and advertising job at a really competitive price.

Original Music

If you need original music for your commercial, film, documentary or TV show, we are soundtrack composers. Take a look at our portfolio and trajectory of more than 30 years in the sector, working for national and regional television channels and producers in programs of all kinds.

Advertising

Looking for music for a TV spot, jingle, or radio commercial? We have a large catalog with more than 40,000 royalty-free music tracks at very competitive prices. Check what kind of license you need for your project to get professional quality music at the best price in the market.
